# Trash icon style credits & licenses

Jetty's Trash tile offers selectable styles (Settings → Appearance → Trash).
Each non-default style bundles an empty and a full image in the asset catalog.
This file records where every piece of bundled artwork came from and under what
terms it is redistributed. Styles not listed here (Seven, Eight, Star, White,
Dark, Cheetah) use artwork provided directly by the project owner.

## Tango

`user-trash.svg` (empty) and `user-trash-full.svg` (full) from the
[Tango icon theme](https://github.com/stephenc/tango-icon-theme), converted to
vector PDF for bundling.

License: Public Domain. The project's COPYING file states verbatim: "The icons
in this repository are herefore released into the Public Domain."

## Pixel

Composited from the "FREE Pixel Art Trash Pack - Animated" by
[KARSIORI STUDIO](https://karsiori.itch.io/free-pixel-art-trash-pack-animated)
(dumpster sprites + trash pile). Credit is not required by the license; given
gladly.

License: CC0 / Public Domain. The pack's page states it may be used "however
you want, in both commercial and non-commercial projects, with no
restrictions."

## Amiga, Atari, GEOS

Original pixel-art homages drawn from scratch for Jetty (see the empty/full
pairs in the asset catalog). They evoke the general wastebasket metaphors and
color palettes of the Amiga Workbench, Atari ST GEM/TOS, and Commodore GEOS
desktops without reproducing any original bitmap: no artwork was extracted,
traced, or copied from those systems, whose icons remain the property of their
respective rights holders. Style names are used descriptively.
